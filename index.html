Welcome to Banting's!

We are one of the largest retail nurseries in the south; our four acre sales yard allows us to provide large quantities at low prices. Unlike large discount chain stores, plants are our only business. We are commited to bringing you the best quality, selection and knowledge of plants in our area.

Our friendly staff is always available to answer your questions. We strive to stay abreast of current research in the field of horticulture. We make it our business to be alert to new plants and trends in our field. Our experience and knowledge allow us to offer plants that do well in our region.

Please stop by Banting’s Nursery at 3425 River Road, Bridge City or at 26300 Highway 190 in Lacombe on the Northshore and rediscover how Banting’s came to be your regional source for plants and nursery supplies.
