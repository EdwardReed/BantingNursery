Welcome to Banting's!

Banting's has been New Orleans' leading retail nursery for over fifty years. Our first location was established in 1960 in Bridge City, on the west bank of the Mississippi River, and in March 2014 we opened our second location in Lacombe, just north of Lake Ponchartrain. We are committed to offering both the north and south shores of the New Orleans area a wide variety of quality trees, shrubs, and bedding plants well-suited for our climate and growing zones.

Our friendly staff is always ready to answer your questions, share their wealth of horticultural knowledge, and help you make the best decisions for your garden.
